    About Us

    Sun Tech is a leading IT company in Nigeria which provides web and mobile solutions to businesses. It is our job to give your business an online presence and connect it to all your potential clients world wide. We also train developers to become world class web and mobile app specialists.

    Our Vision

    To create quality value-added web and mobile application design services for our clients and produce world class web programmers to solve the growing programming need in the world.
